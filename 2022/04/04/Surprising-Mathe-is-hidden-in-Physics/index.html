Surprising - Mathe is hidden in Physics

Physics connects with Math

I find an interesting question from the internet. The question is there are two masses. The left one is at rest with a mass of 1kg and the right one has a mass of 100**n kg with a speed of 10 m/s. Also, there is a wall on the left of the left mass. The question is how many collisions are there including mass colliding mass and mass colliding wall. (All collisions are elastic)

I use Python to solve this question. First, we just need to define each object here. Then, we use conservation of momentum and conservation of energy to modify the collision. Also, the number of collisions will change as the mass of left mass changes(100**n kg).

The result for different n is shown.
