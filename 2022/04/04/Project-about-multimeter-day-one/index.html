Project about multimeter day one

Multimeter-day1

Today, it is the first day for I to design a multimeter by myself.

Today, I draw the electricity circuit which is used to measure voltage and current in a multimeter.

First, I need to understand how traditional current meter works.

Generally, the basic principle is that when there is current passing through the coil, the coil will experience a force in a magnetic field so that the needle will deflect. And when we design the circuit do not forget that the coil has its resistance.

In addition, in a normal multimeter, each part has different measurement range. For example, if you want to measure current, there is a level for current of maximum 5A or maximum 10A. Before we start measuring current, we have to choose the right level or it might be out of range or too small to be measured.

This is the final circuit I draw for measuring current. It has different measuring range and do not forget the coil itself has resistance. In the diagraph, the current meter is coil and the source is the current that we want to measure.

The same principle is applied for measuring voltage. But there is one important thing, if we measure current, resistors must be connected in parallel to divide current. However, in measuring voltage, the have to connect resistors in series to divide voltage.

  • The last thing for today is to combine this two circuit. I choose to use a special switch like this.
  • This is the final circuit. When we want to alternate from measuring current to voltage, we just need to alternate the switch.
