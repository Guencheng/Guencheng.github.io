学自然科学的理由和如何证明自己是面试官要找的学生

First, I want to talk about my original intentions to learn physical science. Since I was in my middle school, I have been interested in Physics, Chemistry and Maths. The most important two points that these subjects attract me is that I can explore the different relationships among different matters and I can look for the natures of different phenomenons. Moreover, many techniques today are based on physical science so, if I want to get an ideal job, the knowledge of physical science is very essential. Then, I would like to tell you about why I am an ideal student in this area. The first I want to mention is that my strong interest in this subject will make me keep working hard in this area. Also, I have had many important qualifications required in this area. For example, I am always keen to try new things and I love innovation. What’s more, I will not just pay attention to academic work. For my perspective, all things around the world are worth studying. And I always prefer to link my academic work with practical applications. During my normal study, I pay a lot attention to experiments because I reckon experiment will reflect the most real situations.
