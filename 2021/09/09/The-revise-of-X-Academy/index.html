The revise of X Academy

​ The revise of X Academy

On the thirteenth of August, I successfully finished my activity in X Academy. In these 12 days, I learnt both computer skills and learning skills. This article aims to discuss what I learn and what I thought in these 12 days.

​ First, I would like to discuss the people I saw in X Academy. The people that impressed me most were my academic leaders. I first met them in the hotel. One of them study in MIT and one of them study in Stanford. I was deeply surprised by their universities. Before, I thought these universities are far away from me. But now, I know that there are many powerful people around me and I need to learn from them. Another thought I got in these 12 days was that I need to widen my horizons. I mean, I can not just concentrate my view in my school. Maybe, in my school, I was not bad. However, if I widen my horizons to the whole world, I could find that there are still a lot for me to do. The people I met in X Academy had all kinds of personalities and they have their own strong points. They are also my competitors so I also need to compare them with me to make me better. Also, I found that there are so many good students in this day so I should think about how to make me stand out of them.

​ Secondly, it is about my learning skills. During these 12 days, the most useful thing I learnt was how to study by myself. Before, if I meet problems, I would ask my teachers for help. This is good obviously. However, I should know that teacher would not appear every time so I had to learn how to study alone. Before, my only path to solve difficult problems was to ask my teacher for help. But now, I can use the Internet as my another useful partner. Generally speaking, I can not depend on others to study any more, I have to learn to find resources and solve problems by myself.
